A new moon heralds a chance for a fresh start, so in regard to your home life, a new chapter is quickly about to form. You may be moving or renovating, closing on a house, or selling your childhood home. If you are getting divorced, your spouse may be moving out (but not without a negotiation), or it may be that your trusted roommate suddenly announces she or he has a new place to live. Alternatively, you may decide to move in with your significant other. If your landlord sold the building, you will be moving out, and that won’t be news you’ll want to hear—unless the landlord offers a generous amount of money to help you accomplish your move.

This eclipse will turn your attention to a female friend who is close to you. The moon is your ruler, and with good-fortune Jupiter conjunct this eclipse, a friend may make a generous gesture that will warm your heart. Venus will play the lead here, and Uranus, the planet of surprise will send Venus a glittering beam. You may travel with a friend, or October 28 may be when you soon return home, happy you took this particular trip together.

Eclipses bring closure, though, and sometimes a lunation like this will “eclipse out” a person from your day-to-day life. There are ways you can remain in touch, and with Saturn sending blessings to the full moon and also to the Sun, you may beat the odds and enjoy your friendship forever. Keep in mind that this may not be the way the October 28 eclipse will work out. You may simply travel or create another plan together and enjoy it to the fullest.
