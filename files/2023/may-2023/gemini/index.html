This month starts out with a difficult full moon—actually a full moon lunar eclipse—in Scorpio, 15 degrees, on May 5. This is not a time to take any risks or proceed with any major launches or initiations in your personal or work life. It would be best not to travel this month. Plan to stay at home at least for the first two weeks of May for you may have to direct others on how to help solve a situation, and that’s always easier if you are home.

You have a strong buildup of planets in your twelfth house of secrets, so it appears either you, or someone near you, is acting on a plan that will affect you, but he or she is keeping things under wraps. To say this more clearly, someone might not be working to your benefit, so keep your antenna up. You will have an extraordinary gift of intuition now and in the months to come, and this will come in handy and be quite correct.

This full moon lunar eclipse will occur in your sixth house of work-a-day assignments and will include workers who report to you at home and at work (such as a nanny, housekeeper, or your assistant, for example). Something is about to reach a crest. Uranus, a planet that covers all things unexpected, in Taurus will be tightly conjunct the Sun and Mercury, and all will directly oppose the full moon in Scorpio. This means news will come out of the blue, and this news would be the last thing you would ever expect to hear—it is a nerve-jangling time. Your ruler Mercury is involved prominently in this eclipse, which spells greater tension. Your home or one of your family members might be in a jam, and you may need to help.
