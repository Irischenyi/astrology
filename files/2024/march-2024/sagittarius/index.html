Your Horoscope for Sagittarius

by Susan Miller

Lately you’ve had people to see and places to go, mainly for work, but for some personal reasons, too. Mars went into Aquarius last month on February 13, and ever since, you may have been traveling a bit more than usual, but only going short distances. I know you prefer to travel long distances to foreign countries, or at the very least to a distant city in your own country, but that’s not in your outlook for now.

You will see that as the month progresses, events will keep pulling your attention back home. Migrating planets on the move in March will be in a hurry to get you to focus on your fourth house of home and family. You will start to see evidence of this just after the new moon arrives on March 10—and its emphasis can last for weeks and months.

The new moon on March 10 is in Pisces, 20 degrees, and you will also have the Sun, Neptune, and Saturn in Pisces. Saturn’s presence seems to suggest you are willing to do whatever it takes to reach a home-related goal, such as buying a house or condo. You might sacrifice pleasures, such as dining out, buying an expensive handbag, or taking vacations, in order to make this happen.

You may hope to make a radical change to your own living quarters by moving to a new house, buying or renting a vacation home, or renovating your current space. Or you could decide to decorate, make small repairs, do maintenance, or simply add a few stylish touches to your home. In any case, you can achieve much in March.
