Your Horoscope for Libra

by Susan Miller

You are working harder than you have in recent memory, for you have a crowd of planets in Pisces and your sixth house of work-a-day projects. Those heavenly bodies are Neptune, Saturn, Mercury, the Sun, and a new moon on March 10—that is a lot of energy in one portion of the chart.

The new moon on March 10 will be in Pisces, 20 degrees, and with so many planets also in Pisces, it appears your work will be creative or artistic in nature. It might seem that all the people involved with your project(s) are calling out your name at once. You will have to be organized to keep everything on time and on budget.

When Mars enters Pisces on March 22 (until April 30), you will be even busier, if that is possible! From Taurus, your eighth house of other people’s money, Jupiter and Uranus will send fat golden beams directly to the Sun and new moon in your sixth house of work projects. This is a sure sign that if you apply yourself to the job at hand, you can do unusually well financially—the best in over six years.

If you work on commission, for bonuses or royalties, or with one-time project fees, you will be raking in money in large quantities. Money may alternatively come from a bank loan, mortgage, or venture capital, or it may come in from other sources like an inheritance, prize winning (such as from a charity raffle), or an insurance premium payout. You may get a tax refund or not have to pay as much as you imagined, or you may be awarded a generous sum from a court case or divorce settlement. The point is, your money seems to come in the form of a one-time payment, rather than a regular salary. The eighth house also rules the benefits you may be approved to receive from your company, so that’s another area to look at—those benefits can be valuable, too.
