Your Horoscope for Leo

by Susan Miller

You have a sterling month ahead, one with variety and opportunities galore. It’s rare to see a month that is near-perfect for you to enjoy—this is it.

Like Gemini and Cancer, as a Leo, all of your transiting planets are above the horizon of your chart, indicating that for most of the month, you will be out in public and more concerned with your career and other activities that involve other people.

Your career is blossoming like a huge hibiscus flower in the sunshine. Jupiter, the good fortune planet that brings growth, happiness, and opportunities, is orbiting close to Uranus, the planet of sudden surprises, and they are both in Taurus, your tenth professional house of honors, awards, and achievement. Jupiter and Uranus will make a historic alignment on April 20 in this same career house and bring some exciting news. You may not even have to wait that long because these two planets are already close. In March get ready for a lucky professional breakthrough that will send your spirits soaring.

You will have Jupiter, the great benefic planet, in Taurus, your house of career, until May 25 of this year.(This trend started in May 2023, and Jupiter won’t be back here until April 2035, more than a decade from now.) Work extra hard while Jupiter is in Taurus, and then from June 2024 onward, you can socialize to your heart’s content. For now, you have a rare competitive edge, where barriers will collapse and allow you to score the victories you deserve.

The Sun is your ruling star, so while everyone will love this day, you will love it more and benefit from it more than most. This day would be superb for a confidential meeting with a VIP to discuss money or to pitch a client ornegotiate a raise or sale.
