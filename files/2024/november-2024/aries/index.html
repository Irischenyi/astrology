Your Horoscope for Aries

by Susan Miller

The month starts off with an important new moon in Scorpio on November 1 to light your financial eighth house of other people’s money. This new moon will receive a magnificent beam from Saturn, the planet that brings stability, and in this case, Saturn has the ability to bring you a sense of financial fulfillment and a long-term sense of security. In the Aries solar chart, Saturn rules your tenth house of career. VIPs seem quite impressed with your performance, so whatever you are doing is clearly hitting the mark.

Pluto will be the lead planet at this time, and for the first time since 1921-1922, Pluto will make a gorgeous, harmonious trine to Uranus. (This aspect will not be exact until July 18, 2026). Still, these two powerhouse planets will be within four degrees of an exact trine, bringing great harmony, so you will feel the help from Pluto. All this means that you will have the ability to make a great deal of money. Of course, too much emphasis on money is not wise, but considering inflation, this is excellent news.

If anyone owes you money, you are likely to receive it this month. It might be a bonus, commission, royalty, or insurance payout, if you filed a claim. It could be money you receive from a judgment in a court case. If you want to buy a house, this new moon will help you make an application for a mortgage with every reason to expect a later approval from the bank. If you want to start a business, you should make a pitch for venture capital or a business line of credit. If you want to go to college or graduate school, a scholarship or generous financial aid may be granted to you. At the same time if you owe money or plan to make an expensive purchase, you will likely be doing so in early November.
