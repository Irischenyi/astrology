A promotion, new job, or new venture in your own business can now come up, along with a chance to make a more generous salary.

Just to clarify, Saturn was designated as your ruler before Uranus was discovered on March 13, 1781. Once Uranus was discovered, the astrological community came together to decide which zodiac sign Uranus would be most appropriate to rule. Because Uranus is tied to future innovation, experimentation, independence, creativity in high tech, and mathematics and scientific exploration, astrologers worldwide chose Aquarius as the sign most fitting to be ruled by Uranus. A ruling planet will always be given extra weight and importance in that sign’s horoscope. Modern astrologers look at both Saturn and Uranus when deciding your Aquarius forecast.
