Your Horoscope for Sagittarius

by Susan Miller

This month starts out slowly and quietly, and for that you will be grateful. You’ve had a lot of activity in your life, so the new moon in Scorpio that occurs on November 1 will bring you a chance to catch your breath and think about all you would like to accomplish in your coming birthday year. A new moon is not influential for just a day but for months, and this new moon is outstandingly special. I will say, however, that a new moon is especially powerful at the time when it appears and for the 10 days that follow.

This new moon lights your twelfth house, a house of healing, rest, and at times, confinement. In the case of confinement, this sector rules hospitals, nursing homes, rehab centers, physical therapy clinics, and other areas of care. If you need a surgery, you might want to schedule the operation for a date during the first two weeks of November. This will be a good, strong month, but we are heading into a slew of retrogrades from the smaller “personal” planets. These are the ones that orbit closest to earth and that we feel strongest when they retrograde—Mercury, Mars, and Venus.

First the little “Dennis the Menace” planet Mercury will go retrograde from November 25 to December 15, throwing a monkey wrench into your holiday shopping and shipping. (Mercury rules commerce, transportation, and shipping.) It is never wise to sign a contract or buy any items with electronic chips or mechanical gears inside during Mercury retrograde. This would probably not be a good time for elective surgery either, unless it is urgent, and for that you should do it immediately—your doctor can make that call. However, the Sun in Sagittarius will warm the healing center of your chart, so this would be an excellent time to not only see a medical doctor but also a trained therapist if you need to talk over a problem or become free of a substance that is hurting your health.

Saturn will be in ideal communication with the November 1 new moon, so the progress you make will remain with you—Saturn gives slow but sure, step-by-step progress that lasts. Since Saturn is traveling through your family sector, if you have been troubled by a relationship with a family member, Saturn is urging you to seek psychological help. Afterward, you would feel so much freer and lighter.

Saturn is also about to help you in a bigger way—he will both boost the effectiveness of the new moon on November 1 and go direct on November 15 (coincidently, this is the same day as this month’s full moon). Saturn has been retrograding since June 29. Saturn will see that your property -and family-related matters show excellent progress starting after November 15.

On another topic, you may have been going through an expensive period, but as soon as Mars leaves for Leo on November 3, to stay until January 6, 2025, you will see your spending drop. Mars in Leo is a fine place for him to be because the Red Planet will light up your ninth house of distant travel—your very favorite activity. Leo is a fire sign like your Sagittarius Sun sign, so Mars will blend beautifully with your Sun and make travel much more possible in the weeks ahead. However, I would avoid taking any trips over the full moon period from November 15 to November 19 because costs could mount up unexpectedly with no way to avoid them.

I mentioned earlier that we are due to run into a number of retrograde planets, so let’s go back and pick up that thread of the conversation and continue. You already know Mercury is about to retrograde later this month from November 25 to December 15. No planet jumps back to its former strength the minute it goes direct, and in the case of Mercury, he will return to full alertness by January 2, 2025.

No dream is too big, dear Sagittarius, to start working on. You have the talent, and opportunity is coming. Success is within your grasp.

Mercury’s big brother Mars will also go retrograde and overlap part of his little brother’s retrograde. Mars will retrograde from December 6, 2024, to February 23, 2025—a very long time. With Mars out of phase, it will be harder to muster the motivation to pull out all the stops and perform at your highest level. For those who are determined, it is possible to do so, but it will take a lot more energy than usual. Mars retrograde is not a great time to date someone entirely new, but if you have been dating someone for a long while, it’s fine.

Just when Mars returns to full strength, it will be Venus’ turn to go retrograde from March 1, 2025, to April 12, 2025. Venus rules love, affection, luxury, beauty, and all the steps you take to become more attractive. Venus won’t be back to her former strength until May 16, 2025. Hold off on making a dramatic change to your appearance or the way you wear your hair, and avoid expensive treatments during this period.

As you see, it would be best to act on important activities in November—before you get all tangled up in these planets’ retrogrades. I will help you through the coming months, for each planet rules a different part of life, so you will know which tasks you can easily accomplish and which ones you should be careful to avoid. Retrograde planets let us reflect and review past decisions and actions to help us see what worked well and what did not—and why.

Now let’s turn to look at the full moon on November 15 in Taurus, 24 degrees. This full moon will light your sixth house, which rules your health and work. If you have gone for tests and screenings, you would likely receive the results within five days of this full moon, between November 15 and November 19.
