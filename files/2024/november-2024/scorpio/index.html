The universe has planned a lovely birthday gift for you—a new moon in Scorpio, 10 degrees, on November 1. Every month I tell you which area of life is going to be your focus, which is based partly on the area where the new moon falls. But when you have a new moon in your own sign, you can use that new moon to improve any area of your life. You will be given an open gift card. Where in your life would you like to make the most progress? What would you like to achieve in the coming birthday year? You may have been uprooted or turned upside down due to some of the eclipses—the wild cards of astrology—in 2022 and 2023.

I love that Saturn in Pisces will be perfectly supportive to this new moon in Scorpio on November 1. That tells me the actions you take will have weight and long-term stability. You are a fixed sign and don’t like constant change—you are hardwired to crave things to stay “as is” unless there is a good reason to change, but alas, life does not always work that way.

If you are single and hoping to find romance, you might find love in early November. Love is serious to you, as evidenced by Saturn in your fifth house. This month Saturn will be in a highly supportive angle to the new moon, so you could find genuine, lasting love. Lately, you have been mindful of using your time well and not squandering it with someone who is not suitable for a committed relationship.
