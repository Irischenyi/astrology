Saturn will also be smiling on this new moon, bringing you enduring affection and a chance to bond with the ones you love. This energy also means that decisions you make regarding romance or children are likely to be solid, long-lasting, and very satisfying. Having Saturn on your side is a big plus.

Single Cancers might meet someone new, and if so, he or she might remain with you quite a while, most likely because you are fascinated by this person, and they treat you well.

Turn away from your computer, dear Cancer, stand up, and dress up, for in November it’s time to play. If you’ve ever had to convince your partner to come along with you, that won’t be the case this time—your other half will gleefully want to join you.

Mars has been in Cancer since September 4 but will move to Leo on November 3, which means you will be spending more money than usual, and this trend will continue until January 6. You may already know what your upcoming expenses will be.

Mars will retrograde next month on December 6 until February 23, and that will make it harder to show productivity at work. Clients will be slow to make a commitment, and even after Mars moves direct, he will not regulate his orbit until April 30. This is one aspect that will affect everyone of every sign to a large degree, although you in particular have a higher propensity to feel the financial aspect of Mars’ position.
