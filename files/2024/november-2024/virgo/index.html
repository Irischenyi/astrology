Your Horoscope for Virgo

by Susan Miller

Last month you had a great deal of evidence that your source of income may be in the process of changing, due to the presence of the new moon solar eclipse on October 2. At the same time, you may have heard more good news about how well you are doing in your career. At work, you stay focused on the job at hand, which is the reason you are so lauded. Your ability to handle details is unsurpassed by the rest of the zodiac.

All that concentration can be exhausting, and although you are doing great in your career, it’s time for a change of scene. Travel is written all over your chart in the first half of November, so if you want to go on a trip, you will find a way. The new moon on Friday, November 1, in Scorpio at 10 degrees will make your travel dream possible. It will likely be to a location within 200 miles, but no matter—getting out of town will feel good. You seem to be going for personal reasons—perhaps to see a sweetheart or enjoy the changing colors of nature—but I cannot be sure about that. If you go for business, it will likely be to pitch a creative idea to a client.

The new moon of November 1 will put Pluto, which is in Capricorn, in charge, but Pluto will be in direct opposition to Mars, positioned across the sky in the last degree of Cancer. This can be a difficult aspect, so if you do travel to see a sweetheart, avoid making controversial statements, and aim for harmony. Pluto opposite Mars can be explosive. If you travel to see a client, be sure you have done all your homework and have all the facts, as well as the history of the client, before you try to negotiate.

The problem is the client is apt to make demands you are not ready to meet. If you find yourself in a difficult, deadlocked situation, ask for more time to consider your client’s demands, and assure your client you will run his or her request by the office. Breaking the chain of discussion may help, for that’s when creative ideas tend to surface.

All your travel this month will likely be done for fun, love, and relaxation, so follow any instincts to take some time away.
