I cannot see all that is happening in your own chart, but I can see the condition of the Sun and moon. The new moon is putting strong emphasis on your marriage or business partner and on jointly held money (money accrued in a marriage or business), taxes and tax refunds, insurance payments, investments, inheritances (along with wills and estate planning), credit card statements, loans, mortgages, commissions, bonuses, royalties, valuable gifts, court cases, and settlements about money, and other funds that come to you outside of salary.
