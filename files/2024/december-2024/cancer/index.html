by Susan Miller

Life is busy in December with a lot of details to cover. Both at home and at work, there will be people to see, places to go, and things to get done.

The good news is that benefic Jupiter is taking a dominant role during this time and will be friendly to the new moon. You are ruled by the moon—this is a good sign that you will like what transpires. Good-fortune Jupiter will keep you cheerful and ready to handle anything life throws your way. You’ll just laugh and say, “Bring it on!”

Mars is ready to help you from his perch in your second house of earned income. This tells me that the work you will be doing will represent a hefty amount of business, so if you are self-employed or work in sales, that should add up to money for you. If you work for others, you may be able to get a temporary part-time job as another way of generating side income.

The sixth house, so lit up for you at the start of the month, would also be the time to get your annual physical exam. Not only is it a good time to check your cholesterol, blood pressure, and so forth, it’s also time to get your eye exam, schedule a dental cleaning, and maybe work on other screenings, such has a prostate exam for guys or a mammogram for women. You know the tests you are supposed to have—now is the time to check them off your list so you can start the New Year in great condition. Spending an hour or two at the doctor’s office can mean the difference between a long or short life.
