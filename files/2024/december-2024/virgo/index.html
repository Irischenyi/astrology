Your December 2024 Horoscope for Virgo

by Susan Miller

Your career has occupied much of your thinking since benefic Jupiter entered your tenth house of prestigious professional advancement and began to stir up exciting possibilities. This is the first time in 12 years that Jupiter has spent time in this area of your chart, having entered on May 25, 2024, and this lucky planet is set to stay until June 9, 2025.

No wonder your attention has been riveted on your career—these have been some of the best and most positive months you have had in years to generate career growth and admiration for your accomplishments, from both peers and VIPs. It appears that you have been putting your time to good use.

This month, however, you will lavish time and attention on your adoring family. You are likely to notice this trend from the moment you enter December, thanks to the new moon in Sagittarius, 10 degrees, which will light your fourth house of home (or accent other property you may own, rent, or want to sell, renovate, or decorate). This same area of your chart rules your parents (including adoptive parents), as well as other members of your immediate family. This new moon will appear on November 30 or December 1, depending on your time zone.

Jupiter will have an important role to play at this time, and happily he is found orbiting opposite the new moon and Sun. This friendly partnership adds a generous, optimistic, and warm vibration to this new moon.

Saturn, however, is in hard angle to the new moon, which could create a little difficulty with your sweetheart—he or she might not want to spend the holidays with your family. There are other possible scenarios, but you may have an inkling of what sticky topic might come up with a partner. But Mars is ducking into a phone booth, putting on his cape, and emerging as Superman. As he zooms through space, Mars will send a brilliant, positive beam to the new moon and Sun. This makes me feel that you will be able to win over your partner with gentle persuasion.

Your December 2024 Horoscope for Virgo

by Susan Miller

Last month your ruling planet Mercury went retrograde on November 25, and he will go direct on December 15. Mercury will be retrograde in Sagittarius in your home and family sector, so you may be of two minds concerning just about everything. You will waffle back and forth, but this is normal—you will feel better once Mercury settles down mid-month.

No planet returns immediately to its previous energetic state after retrograding. Instead, step by step the planet grows stronger, and in the case of Mercury, he will regain his full influence by January 2.

Mercury’s big brother Mars will also go retrograde this month. We are not used to seeing Mars in retrograde because he only does this every two years. This time Mars will retrograde on December 6 and stay in retrograde until February 23, a period of a little over 11 weeks! That’s a long time. It will take Mars longer than usual to get back to full power, until April 30.

The positive developments in your career mid-month may be the best gift Santa could have ever delivered and placed under your tree.
