Your June 2024 Horoscope for Capricorn

by Susan Miller

In this very busy month, you will need to bring your A-game. With five out of 10 heavenly bodies in your sixth house of work assignments, you will need to stay organized and alert for new clients, and customers will be beating a path to your door. This all may be surprising to you if you expected a sleepy, quiet June, but having people clamoring for your goods and services is always a good thing. It may be doubly good news if you are self-employed. Not only will clients be streaming in the door, but phones will be ringing and a motherload of emails will be pouring into your inbox.

What has changed, you may wonder? The biggest change is that late last month, on May 25, Jupiter, the planet of goodness, growth, good luck, and happiness moved into a new sign of Gemini and has since settled into your sixth house of work assignments. It is customary for several planets to gather together for a welcoming party, and those invited to this month’s celebration for Jupiter are Venus, Mercury, the Sun, and the new moon. All these guests will be in air-sign Gemini, so things will move fast (unlike last month, where progress was very slow). The new moon on June 6 will appear in Gemini, 16 degrees, and will set everything into motion.

Last month a crowd of planets gathered in Taurus and waved goodbye to wish Jupiter a safe voyage to his new placement in Gemini. Jupiter takes 12 years to rotate through all 12 constellations, spending one year in each sign. Jupiter had not been in Gemini (and your solar sixth house) for a dozen years.

You will not lack for projects and assignments now that Jupiter is in Gemini. In fact, you may have to hire people to help you get all the work done. Jupiter brings opportunity but also the means to take advantage of the opportunity, so you will likely get approval for hiring temporary or permanent workers. Sometimes it’s hard to find qualified people you can trust, but not this time—you will find plenty of perfect candidates who will display a solidly good work ethic and who will be enthusiastic about the job.
