by Susan Miller

You have an urge to merge, dear Sagittarius, and the person you have chosen must be mighty special. Sagittarius is known to be slow to commit—you are very careful about forming any sort of partnership, especially marriage—but June surely seems like the perfect month for you to do so. You will have five out of 10 heavenly bodies in Gemini, your seventh house, which rules marriage and serious business collaborations and partnerships.

Among the planets crowded in your seventh house is the good fortune planet Jupiter, your ruler. It took Jupiter 12 years to make his journey to this area of your chart, and he is set to be here for a full year, until June 9, 2025. All throughout this time, Jupiter will bring you his blessings of happiness and growth.

Along with lucky Jupiter, the crowd of planets in your seventh house of marriage and partnerships are Venus (happiness), Mercury (communication and contractual agreements), the Sun (determination and will), and the June 6 new moon (opportunity) at 16 degrees, all in your opposite sign of Gemini.

Gemini is a breezy, light, fast-moving sign that will blend beautifully with the fire-sign element of your Sun in Sagittarius. Gemini is an air sign that will make your spirited fire-sign personality burn more brightly.
