Your June 2024 Horoscope for Scorpio

by Susan Miller

June will bring lots of attention to money. In a stroke of luck, good-fortune Jupiter has now settled into your eighth house of other people’s money for the first time in 12 years. Apparently, you are about to do some savvy money management. You may be ready to launch a big dream—buy a house, renovate or fix up a home, go back to college, get married, or take a trip of a lifetime. These types of lifestyle changes often require large sums, so this might describe your situation now.

You have half the solar system crowded in your eighth house, which is naturally ruled by Scorpio. This gives you an inborn talent for making good choices in finding ways to get the most from the money you have and to make it grow. If you have not yet become cognizant of this innate talent, partner with a trustworthy financial advisor and look at your options. If you are ready to make a big purchase, such as real estate, a renovation, a car, or any other large asset, find out the smartest way to go about getting the money you need at the lowest interest rate (while avoiding unnecessary taxes, such as if you have to sell stock and be hit with capital gains).

The new moon on June 6 in Gemini, 16 degrees, will trigger financial talks because this new moon is joined in Gemini by the Sun, Venus, Mercury, and Jupiter. In addition, Pluto will send a beam to Jupiter from your fourth house of home. This is a spectacular trine (heavenly aspect) that will happen exactly on Sunday, June 2.

Both Jupiter and Pluto orbit like little snails, inching their way along their appointed paths, and they will still be in significant mathematical closeness to each other during the new moon on June 6, a fabulously lucky aspect that’s known to bring discussions about large sums of money. Since Jupiter and Pluto will be supremely friendly, there’s a good chance you will get the money you need. If you are looking for a job, the salary and benefits package being offered and negotiated is likely to please you. (Your second house of earned income is ruled by Jupiter, and Jupiter is an active, helpful participant here.)
