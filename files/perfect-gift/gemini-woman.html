gemini-woman

Lily-of-the-Valley is one of Gemini’s favorite flowers, so send her a nosegay or give her soaps, lotions and cologne in that scent. Her need to communicate is strong–find her an elegant pen (rollerball or fountain pens are good) or go with her to a fine shop to select personal stationary for her on the finest fiber paper, compete with a printer’s die for her engraved initials. She would love a personal fax machine, beeper, or a purse-size cellular phone to help her stay in touch.

She also likes new accessories to change her look, so buy her a new thin belt with sterling silver buckle, a small leather backpack (instead of a purse) by Prada or a fashionable piece of costume jewelry that she chooses with you. She is on the go a lot, so the chances are, she needs new luggage. Make it one of those new pieces with rollers on the bottom, or a handsome carry-on bag for weekend getaways.

Your Gemini girl likes to do two things at once, so get her wireless, cordless radio/head phones set to listen to while she works. Or surprise her with a little bitty reading light to use on train or when a passenger in a car at night. She loves to write, so give her a beautifully bound diary, with lock and key. Finally, a Stuben glass paperweight would be an elegant addition to her pretty glass desk.
